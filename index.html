Sea The Future

An elite, integrated marketing campaign positioning the Royal Beach Club Cozumel as the ultimate signature highlight of the modern cruise journey.

Explore the Strategy

A Seamless Transition

Elevating the Shore Experience

Royal Beach Club Cozumel is a luxury, all-inclusive destination designed exclusively for cruise guests. We are extending the onboard luxury experience directly to the shore with private beaches, swim-up bars, upscale dining, and premium cabanas.

Our strategy targets luxury-focused travelers and families, turning a standard port stop into a must-book destination, driving awareness up by 30% and bookings by 25%.

Campaign Activities

Global World Cup Promo

Capitalizing on the world's most-watched sporting event with $1M in broadcast ads and digital screens in major international transit hubs to drive maximum visibility.

The Weeknd Tour Integration

A $500K star-powered partnership that places the Beach Club at the center of global pop culture, converting massive buzz into direct bookings.

Influencer Cruise Experience

Collaborating with 10-15 top-tier creators for authentic, first-person storytelling, featuring interactive TikTok reels and highly engaging cross-platform giveaways.

A-List Endorsements

Leveraging premium talent including Selena Gomez, Zendaya, Kevin Hart, and Johnny Depp to showcase diverse experiences from cinematic luxury to high-energy vibes.

Interactive Guest Polling

Gamifying the vacation experience. Guests use the mobile app to vote on daily activities, actively shaping their own beach club environment.

Promotional Bundling

A strategic, cost-effective add-on methodology that pairs Beach Club access with popular cruise packages to lower hesitation and drive overall cart value.
