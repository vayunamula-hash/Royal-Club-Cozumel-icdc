The Destination & Objective

A Seamless Cruise-to-Shore Transition

Royal Beach Club Cozumel is a luxury, all-inclusive beach destination exclusively for cruise guests. It extends the onboard luxury experience with private beaches, swim-up bars, upscale dining, premium cabanas, curated activities, and live entertainment.

Campaign Goals:

  • Boost awareness by 30% and bookings by 25% in 45 days.
  • Engage 100,000+ potential customers via digital channels.
  • Position the beach club as a high-value, limited-time upgrade.

Campaign Activities

FIFA World Cup Global Promotion

Highlighting Royal Beach Club Cozumel during the world's most-watched sporting event with $1M in broadcast ads and digital screens in major transit hubs.

The Weeknd Tour Partnership

A star-powered endorsement placing the Beach Club at the center of global pop culture. Generates massive buzz and builds immediate credibility among trend-driven travelers.

Influencer Cruise Experience

Collaborating with 10-15 top-tier creators for authentic, first-person storytelling. Features interactive content, TikTok reels, and cross-platform giveaways.

Celebrity Endorsements

Leveraging A-list talent including Selena Gomez, Zendaya, Kevin Hart, and Johnny Depp to showcase premium dining, cinematic luxury, and high-energy beach club vibes.

Interactive Guest Polling

Gamifying the cruise experience. Guests use the Royal Caribbean app to vote on daily activities like DJ parties, steel drums, and dining themes, shaping their own vacation.

Promotional Incentives

Strategic, cost-effective bundling that pairs Beach Club access with popular cruise add-ons to drive conversion, lower hesitation, and increase overall booking revenue.
